The Guardian Of One Wing Angel

By Rico Janto

7 September 2022

The journey towards armageddon comes with obstacles

As the war rages on

Many guardians are fighting for purposes

Some for power, others for freedom

One warrior is fighting for vengeance

His guardian angel died in the battlefield

He lost any purpose in life

She sacrifice herself for him to fight forward

Her death shattered his inner state mine

The angel loses her wing, but the warrior gains a demon spirit

A demon spirit that woke his rage to go to the battlefield

Rebuilding a kingdom that he once build

Inflicting a retribution for her killer as well as his sin

Fight for justice, fight for a new life, fight for a new path and destiny

Make it impenetrable, unite the guardians as a comrade, MARCH as this is your last stand.
